Namsan Seoul Tower

Namsan Seoul Tower, officially YTN Seoul Tower, is a landmark observation tower located on Namsan Mountain in central Seoul. It offers breathtaking panoramic views of the city and surrounding areas, especially stunning at night. It's also famous for its 'love locks' where couples attach padlocks to the fences as a symbol of eternal love.

Attractions at Namsan Seoul Tower:

  • Observation Decks
  • Love Locks
  • Restaurants and Cafes
  • Namsan Park trails
