Myeongdong

Myeongdong is one of Seoul's primary shopping and tourism districts, known for its endless rows of cosmetics shops, fashion boutiques, and street food stalls. It's a vibrant area that buzzes with energy both day and night.

Highlights of Myeongdong:

  • Shopping for K-beauty products
  • Street food galore
  • Myeongdong Nanta Theatre
  • Myeongdong Cathedral
