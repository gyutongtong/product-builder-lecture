Gangnam

Gangnam is an upscale and fashionable district in Seoul, famous for its high-end shopping, entertainment, and the global sensation 'Gangnam Style'. It's a symbol of modern South Korea, with towering skyscrapers, luxury boutiques, and vibrant nightlife.

Key attractions include:

  • COEX Mall & Aquarium
  • Starfield Library
  • Bongeunsa Temple
  • Garosu-gil street
