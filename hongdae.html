Hongdae

Hongdae is a district in Seoul known for its youthful ambience, urban arts and indie music culture, driven by the presence of Hongik University. It's a popular spot for live music, unique cafes, street performances, and trendy fashion.

Things to do in Hongdae:

  • Enjoy street performances
  • Explore unique cafes and art markets
  • Visit the Trick Eye Museum
  • Experience vibrant nightlife
