Gyeongbokgung Palace

Gyeongbokgung Palace is the largest of the Five Grand Palaces built during the Joseon Dynasty. It is a majestic symbol of Korean history and culture, offering a glimpse into the royal life of ancient Korea.

Highlights include:

  • Geunjeongjeon (Throne Hall)
  • Gyeonghoeru Pavilion
  • Hyangwonjeong Pond
  • Changing of the Royal Guard ceremony
